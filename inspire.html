My Parents: The True Definition of a Hero

Why are my parents my heroes?

To me. my parents are the perfect example/definition of a hero.

The reason that I admire these two marvelous and beautiful people, is because they are the best role models ever.

Coming from a beautiful little town in Chihuahua, Mexico, they came to the U.S. not knowing the language or having many family members here.

They gave my two older brothers and I, the best life.

While they were not given the opportunity to attend college, they always motivated us to go to college and get a career.

My parents sacrificed many things just,so that we could have what we wanted.

They are one of the top reasons, that I decided to pursue another degree after graduating with a Bachelor's in Biology.

I was given the luxury of being able to become a software engineering major, and that is all thanks to them.

Without their help, I probably would have been able to do it.
